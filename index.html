OUR MENU

KING COBRA
The King Cobra, a long, graceful and deadly predator endemic to Southeast Asia is a beautiful species that is known for being the longest venomous snake in the world.This snake is a devoted parent that moves through the sub-tropical thickets of Southeast Asian forests marking its territory along the way.
LION
The lion is one of the largest predators in the wild. The perfect blend of strength and agility enables the lion to dominate other predators in its habitat.With a group that contains up to 15 intelligent adults, a pride of lions does not fear any other creature in the wild. As designed over a million years of evolution, this predator has no visible weaknesses.
RED FOX
Since the ancient times, the Red Foxes have been hunted by humans, but their adaptability and resourcefulness is such that they still exist in huge numbers today.After conquering the entire northern hemisphere, the species invaded Australia, where it was once introduced by humans.
